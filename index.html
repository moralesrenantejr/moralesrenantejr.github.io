A bit about me

I'm Renante G. Morales Jr., a Software Engineer with 7+ years of experience across full-stack web development, systems migration, and QA/test automation. I'm skilled in PHP (Laravel, Lumen), React, PostgreSQL, and MySQL, with hands-on experience leading legacy-to-modern system migrations and building production software for cooperative and enterprise clients.
